Mysteries of the World:

The world is filled with unexplained mysteries that are yet to be discovered. For centuries these mysteries have evoked a sense of curiosity among scientists and entusiaists. Despite many research and studies, many of these mysteries remain mysteries. These mysteries range from the ancient Great Pyramids of Giza to the unexplained disappearances of ships and planes in the Bermuda Triangle . Some mysteries are natural, some are ancient, some are hidden, and others are difficult to explain, but all of these mysteries urge us to explore, innovate, question, and uncover the unexplained theories of the extraordinary world.
