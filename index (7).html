Mysteries of the World:

The world is full of puzzles that have baffled humans for centuries—enigmas that refuse to be explained and continue to stir our imagination. From the colossal Great Pyramids of Giza, whose builders and methods remain a mystery, to the treacherous waters of the Bermuda Triangle, where ships and planes vanish without a trace, these mysteries challenge our understanding of history, science, and nature. Some are ancient, some are natural, and some are inexplicable—but all awaken a deep curiosity, urging us to explore, question, and uncover the hidden secrets of our extraordinary world.
